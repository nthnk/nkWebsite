Technology and Web Development

My interest for web development began after my roommate introduced me to the idea of using computer science to design websites
and apps. I started learning how to code through online lessons, then by making this website. I'm currently fluent in HTML & CSS with
a good introduction to JQuery and Javascript.

After seeing a job advertisement for Hatch Canada, I decided to give it a shot by applying and attending the group interview. This job
required no coding experience at all, as they said they would teach me all the Javascript I needed to know for the job.

After being hired, I completed the online and in person training in two weeks and began teaching children Javascript through the P5JS
library that Hatch had on their website. It was a great experience that grew my interest for coding for the present and future!

Interests & Hobbies

Throughout my childhood I loved sports and being active. I enjoyed playing and learning all the different sports in Phys-ed class and
with my friends. It seems that everytime I hungout with my friends, all we did was play one sport or another.

I had a brief stint with baseball when I was 6, then moved on to rep soccer at 14. After a few years in soccer, I realized it wasn't for me
and I focused more on Ultimate Frisbee, a sport I began playing in grade 8.

In my senior year of high school, I captained our frisbee team and we finished as city finalists. After realizing how much fun it was
playing alongside my friends and teammates, a few friends and I tried out for the Toronto Elites program, and we were placed on the
Toronto B Team, Uproar. We competed at the Nationals tournament in Edmonton and finished 9th in the country. However, frisbee
hasn't stopped for me as I'm still currently playing on the UWaterloo Ultimate Frisbee squad!
